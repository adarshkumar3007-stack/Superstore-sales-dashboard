Superstore Sales Dashboard | Power BI

Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes Superstore sales performance across categories, regions, products, and profitability metrics. 
The project focuses on identifying high-performing segments, loss-making products, discount impact on profit margins,
and overall business trends to support data-driven decision-making.

Business Objective
The objective of this project was to:
- Analyze overall sales and profitability
- Identify profitable and loss-making products
- Understand the relationship between discounts and profits
- Track regional and category performance
- Discover business trends for strategic decision-making

Tools & Skills Used
- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX Measures
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization
- Business Analysis
- Dashboard Design
- Data Storytelling

KPI Measures
The following DAX measures were created:
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Profit Margin %
- Average Order Value
- Total Quantity
- Total Discount
- Average Discount %

DASHBOARD PAGES
PAGE 1 — EXECUTIVE OVERVIEW
This page provides a high-level business summary through KPI cards and category-level analysis.
Key Insights
- Technology category generated the highest sales and profits.
- West region contributed the highest overall sales.
- Sales showed an upward trend over time with periodic fluctuations.
- Office Supplies delivered strong profits despite lower sales than Technology.

PAGE 2 — DETAILED ANALYSIS
This page focuses on sub-category and product-level analysis.
Key Insights
- Phones and Chairs were the top-selling sub-categories.
- Tables and Bookcases generated negative profits.
- Certain products consistently contributed to business losses.
- A small group of products contributed disproportionately to profits.

PAGE 3 — PROFITABILITY & DISCOUNT ANALYSIS
This page analyzes how discounts affect profitability and identifies operational inefficiencies.
Key Insights
- Higher discount percentages were associated with weaker profitability.
- Tables and Bookcases showed negative or weak profit margins.
- Several heavily discounted products resulted in losses.
- Technology maintained the strongest sales and profit performance overall.

BUSINESS RECOMMENDATIONS:
- Reduce excessive discounting on low-margin products.
- Re-evaluate pricing strategy for Tables and Bookcases.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing Technology products.
- Monitor loss-making products and optimize inventory decisions.
- Expand strategies used in high-performing regions to weaker markets.
